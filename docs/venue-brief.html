Venue 1: Palais Brongniart

Address: Place de la Bourse, 75002 Paris
Metro: Bourse (Line 3) - 2 min walk
Website: https://www.palaisbrongniart.com

Overview

Historic Paris Stock Exchange building, now premier event venue. For ~100 attendees, book one of their conference rooms (200 capacity) rather than the main hall.

Key Features

✅ Prime central location - easy international access
✅ Historical prestige - impressive setting
✅ Professional AV & WiFi included
✅ Multiple breakout rooms available
✅ Proven tech event experience (AI Paris Summit, VivaTech)

Capacity Options

  • Conference Room (recommended): 200 theater / 120 banquet
  • Breakout spaces: 2-3 rooms (50-100 capacity each)
  • Main hall (800): Overkill for 100 attendees

Pricing Estimate (1 day, ~100 people)

  • Conference room rental: €12,000-18,000
  • Technical package: €4,000-6,000
  • Catering (€100/person): €10,000
  • TOTAL: €26,000-34,000

Pros & Cons

✅ Central location, prestige, reliable infrastructure
⚠️ Higher cost, exclusive caterer (Potel & Chabot)

Contact: contact@palaisbrongniart.com | +33 (0)1 83 92 20 20

Venue 2: Grand Palais Éphémère

Address: Champ de Mars, 75007 Paris
Metro: École Militaire (Line 8) - 5 min walk
Website: https://www.rmngp.fr/grand-palais-ephemere

Overview

Modern temporary structure on Champ de Mars with Eiffel Tower views. Main hall (1,500 capacity) far too large for 100 attendees - would need modular section.

Key Features

✅ Iconic Eiffel Tower backdrop
✅ Modern infrastructure (Olympic standard)
✅ Flexible layout options
⚠️ Oversized for small conference
⚠️ Less central location

Capacity Options

  • Modular section (recommended): 200-300 capacity
  • Main hall (1,500): Way too large
  • Mezzanine: VIP/breakout area

Pricing Estimate (1 day, ~100 people)

  • Modular space rental: €15,000-25,000
  • Technical package: €6,000-9,000
  • Catering (€100/person): €10,000
  • TOTAL: €31,000-44,000

Pros & Cons

✅ Eiffel Tower views, modern facilities
⚠️ Oversized, expensive for 100 attendees, less central

Contact: location.ephemere@rmngp.fr | +33 (0)1 40 13 48 00

Venue 3: Station F ⭐ RECOMMENDED

Address: 5 Parvis Alan Turing, 75013 Paris
Metro: Chevaleret (Line 6) - 3 min walk
Website: https://stationf.co

Overview

World's largest startup campus. Event hall (600 capacity) is appropriately sized for 100 attendees - best value and authentic tech atmosphere.

Key Features

✅ Best value - lowest cost
✅ Tech ecosystem - 1,000+ startups on campus
✅ Right-sized - 600-person hall works for 100 attendees
✅ AI credibility - hosts ML Paris, Hugging Face events
✅ Fiber WiFi - startup-grade connectivity
✅ Content studios - podcast/video capabilities

Capacity

  • Main event hall: 600 theater (perfect for 100)
  • Workshop rooms: 10+ spaces (50-200 capacity)
  • Outdoor courtyard: networking space

Pricing Estimate (1 day, ~100 people)

  • Event hall rental: €6,000-10,000
  • Technical package: €2,000-3,000
  • Catering (€75/person): €7,500
  • TOTAL: €15,500-20,500

Pros & Cons

✅ Best value, authentic tech vibe, networking with startups
✅ Right-sized space, proven AI event track record
⚠️ East Paris location (less central), warehouse aesthetic

Contact: events@stationf.co | +33 (0)1 80 97 41 70

Recommendation

Primary: Station F ⭐

For ~100 attendees, Station F offers the best combination of:
- Value: 30-40% cheaper than alternatives
- Authenticity: Real tech ecosystem, not just a rental venue
- Scale: 600-person hall appropriately sized (won't feel empty)
- Networking: Access to 1,000+ startups and tech partners
- Track record: Proven AI/ML event experience

Budget: €15,500-20,500 total (including catering)

Alternative: Palais Brongniart (Conference Room)

If prestige and central location are priorities:
- Book a 200-capacity conference room (not main hall)
- Higher cost but prime location and historic setting
- Better for international attendees unfamiliar with Paris

Budget: €26,000-34,000 total (including catering)

Not Recommended: Grand Palais Éphémère

  • Oversized and overpriced for 100 attendees
  • Better suited for 500+ person events
  • Only consider if you need Eiffel Tower backdrop for marketing
